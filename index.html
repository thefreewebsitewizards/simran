Become a Model – No Matter Your Height

Hi, I'm Simran. I'm a petite, multiethnic model from Nepal who's proudly walked in Melbourne Fashion Week, Melbourne Fashion Festival, and India Fashion Week Australia.

Despite the industry's height standards, I've been called the "Pose Queen" by photographers and featured in major shows—proving that modeling is about confidence, presence, and skill more than inches.

Now, I'm offering a full course for aspiring models—especially petite models who want to break into the industry.

In this course, you'll learn:

  • How to master posing for different shoots (editorial, runway, commercial)
  • Building confidence & embracing your unique features
  • Industry secrets for castings, portfolios & networking
  • How to work with photographers, stylists & brands
  • Social media presence & self-branding

Whether you want to model professionally or simply feel empowered in front of the camera, this course will give you the tools to shine.
